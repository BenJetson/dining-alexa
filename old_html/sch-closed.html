Eat on the run with our Reusable To-Go Box program!
Our program is free to join for meal plan members and only costs $5 per semester for those without a meal plan. Just let a cashier know at The Fresh Food Company or Schilletter that you would like to participate and you will receive a reusable To-Go Box for immediate use. The next time you would like a to-go meal, bring back your used To-Go Box and receive a clean one to use now or a credit for a reusable To-Go Box later. To-Go Boxes are not permitted for dine-in and lids must close.
