Located in building B of Core Campus, The Fresh Food Company features display-style cooking. Choose from made-to-order entrées at the Mongolian Grill, pasta, deli, and omelette stations. Gluten Solutions offers entrées and sides made without gluten containing ingredients. Head upstairs to the 2nd level lounge which features flexible, comfortable seating areas and multiple TV’s with screen sharing capabilities perfect for study groups, presentations and more.

2018-2019 Walk-In Rates: Breakfast: $8.50 plus tax Lunch: $11.50 plus tax Dinner: $12.00 plus tax

Eat on the run with our Reusable To-Go Box program!
Our program is free to join for meal plan members and only costs $5 per semester for those without a meal plan. Just let a cashier know at The Fresh Food Company or Schilletter that you would like to participate and you will receive a reusable To-Go Box for immediate use. The next time you would like a to-go meal, bring back your used To-Go Box and receive a clean one to use now or a credit for a reusable To-Go Box later. To-Go Boxes are not permitted for dine-in and lids must close.
