Schilletter Dining Hall is located on the east side of campus in Bryan Mall. This dining location with display-style cooking stations and made-to-order entrées features: True Balance (station that eliminates 7 of the top 8 allergens), a Mongolian Grill, rotating menus at the Destination Station, a bistro-inspired grill, brick-oven pizza, a salad bar, and more. With recent renovations, the décor features aesthetically pleasing seating, coupled with lighting to create a variety of areas with their own eclectic atmospheres. There is even a fireplace with cozy seating for studying and meetings.

2018-2019 Walk-In Rates: Breakfast: $8.50 plus tax Lunch: $11.50 plus tax Dinner: $12.00 plus tax Sunday Brunch: $11.50 plus tax 

Eat on the run with our Reusable To-Go Box program!
Our program is free to join for meal plan members and only costs $5 per semester for those without a meal plan. Just let a cashier know at The Fresh Food Company or Schilletter that you would like to participate and you will receive a reusable To-Go Box for immediate use. The next time you would like a to-go meal, bring back your used To-Go Box and receive a clean one to use now or a credit for a reusable To-Go Box later. To-Go Boxes are not permitted for dine-in and lids must close.

Accepted Here

Paw Points, Tigerstripe, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and Cash, in addition to meal plan swipes.
